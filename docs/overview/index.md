# Overview

This section is the 10-minute orientation. By the end of it, you should be able to:

- State the difference between **research** and **development** in one sentence.
- Explain why **R&D** is the combination of the two and not a synonym for either.
- Place a piece of biomedical work — a Python script, a paper, a clinical tool — into the right category.
- Decide which part of the rest of the hub to read first.

## What's in this section

- **[What is R&D?](what-is-rd.md)** — the core definition and why it matters that you understand it as a *process*, not a job title.
- **[Research vs Development](research-vs-development.md)** — what each side actually does, with examples from biomedical AI.
- **[R&D vs Research vs Development](comparison.md)** — the comparison table and the worked example you will come back to.
